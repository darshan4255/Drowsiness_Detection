ABOUT

Whether it’s due to medication, a sleep disorder or a poor night’s rest, new research points to the risks and potential dangers of drowsy driving. Missing one to two hours of the recommended seven hours of sleep a night nearly doubles the risk of a car accident. Sleepiness can come without warning, so drivers should prioritize getting enough sleep and avoid driving when they are fatigued. DriveAlert is a solution to this problem as it identifies when the driver is falling asleep and alarms the driver so that appropriate measures can be taken.

The best drivers are aware that they must be beware.
